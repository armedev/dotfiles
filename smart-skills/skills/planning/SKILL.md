## Planning

Before writing any code, take time to understand the problem and plan your approach.

* Read requirements carefully and ask clarifying questions
* Break down the task into small, testable steps
* Identify edge cases, constraints, and potential pitfalls
* Choose appropriate design patterns and data structures
* Consider trade-offs between simplicity and flexibility
* Draft a rough implementation plan or pseudocode
* Estimate effort and identify dependencies
* Write down assumptions and validation criteria
